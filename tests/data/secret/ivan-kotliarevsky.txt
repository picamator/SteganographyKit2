Ivan Petrovych Kotliarevsky (Ukrainian: Іван Петрович Котляревський)
(9 September [O.S. 29 August] 1769 in Poltava – 10 November [O.S. 29 October] 1838 in Poltava, Russian Empire, now Ukraine),
was a Ukrainian writer, poet and playwright, social activist, regarded as the pioneer of modern Ukrainian literature.
